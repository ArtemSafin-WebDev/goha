Order processing

Orders are processed monday–friday and shipped within 3-7 business days. Excluding the weekend and major holidays. Once your order is processed and shipped, you will receive an email notification with your tracking information.

Shipping

For international shipments, we use proven freight forwarders such as TNT, DHL or UPS. In order to handle the journey and protect our products from potential damage, we make sure that they are packed sufficiently.

Our prices don't include shipping costs. They will be added to your order during checkout.

Shipping to Belarus and Iran is not available.

Eligibility for return

You are eligible to return a product within 30 days from the date of delivery for a full refund or exchange. The product must be in its original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags attached. Certain items such as intimate apparel, swimsuits, and final sale items are ineligible for return due to hygiene reasons. Please check the product description for eligibility before purchasing.

Return process

— To initiate a return, please log in to your account on our website, select the order you wish to return, and follow the step-by-step instructions.

— package the item in its original packaging, along with the order invoice or packing slip.

— drop off the package at the nearest authorized shipping carrier or schedule a pickup.
